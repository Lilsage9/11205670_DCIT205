Events page

Welcome to the Events Page of the Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana – your gateway to a vibrant calendar of academic, professional, and community-engaging activities. We believe in fostering a dynamic learning environment that extends beyond the classroom, and our events are curated to provide enriching experiences for students, faculty, and industry professionals alike.

Explore a diverse array of events, including:

Seminars and Workshops

Engage with industry leaders, researchers, and experts through our regular seminars and workshops. These events cover a wide range of topics, from emerging technologies to career development strategies.

Hackathons and Coding Competitions

Sharpen your coding skills and unleash your creativity in our hackathons and coding competitions. These events provide a platform for innovation, collaboration, and friendly competition among our talented student community.

Guest Lectures

Benefit from the wisdom and insights of renowned professionals in the field of computer science through our guest lecture series. These lectures provide a unique opportunity to gain perspectives from industry experts and thought leaders.

Student Showcases

Celebrate the achievements of our students in our periodic showcases. From project exhibitions to demonstrations of cutting-edge technologies, these events highlight the creativity and talent within our community.
